Our Story

More than just a store, we are a community passionate about chess.

Our Journey

Founded in 2020 by a group of chess enthusiasts, our store began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality chess sets to players of all levels. From humble beginnings selling handmade wooden sets, we've grown to offer a curated selection of modern electronic boards, exclusive pieces, and educational resources. We believe chess is more than a game—it's a tool for strategic thinking and personal growth.
